One particular book which is recommended reading is John Grisham. This book is about a lawyer who begins re-evaluating his priorities in life when a bad incident occurs within his law firm. Consequently, he becomes acquianted with the inner city streets, and realizes the harsh existence of a homeless, and vows to give them a chance in the courts.The Street Lawyer is a great book. It is well written and interesting.Other books by John Grisham include The Firm, The Pelican Brief, and The Client.
